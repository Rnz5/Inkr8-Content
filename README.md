## About

**Inkr8-Content** is the public content database for **Inkr8**, a competitive English writing platform for Android.

This repository contains the content used to power Inkr8's writing challenges, including:

- Words
- Themes
- Topics

Inkr8 uses this content to generate writing exercises where users must write short paragraphs using assigned words, themes, and topics.

## Content Philosophy

Every piece of content should support Inkr8's identity:

- Useful for English learners
- Strong enough for writing practice
- Clear in meaning and usage
- Organized by difficulty, rarity, theme, or topic
- Suitable for AI evaluation by R8
- Built around interesting, relevant, and challenging ideas

Inkr8 is designed to make users **apply vocabulary**, not just recognize it.

The topics and themes are designed around ideas that matter in today's society: technology, philosophy, psychology, ethics, culture, education, science, and other areas that can push users to think while they write.

The goal is to make each writing challenge feel intellectually demanding, socially relevant, and useful for real expression.

## About Inkr8

**Inkr8** is a competitive AI-powered writing platform where users improve their English writing by submitting short paragraphs, receiving scores from **R8**, earning **Merit**, climbing ranked leagues, and competing in tournaments.

Unlike passive learning apps, Inkr8 turns writing improvement into a competitive system.

Writing well gives status.

---

## R8

**R8** is the AI evaluator and mascot of Inkr8.

He evaluates writing based on:

- Grammar
- Coherence
- Vocabulary usage
- Required word usage
- Creativity
- Structure
- Depth
- Expression

R8 is sharp, sarcastic, and honest. He is not designed to give empty praise.
